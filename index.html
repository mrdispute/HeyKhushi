Khushi's Story

A gamer who just wants to have fun 🎮

Hey there! I'm Khushi, a full-time content creator who's passionate about gaming and building a chill community. Whether I'm streaming Valorant clutches, cruising through GTA Roleplay, or getting eliminated in Fall Guys — it's always about the vibes, not the wins!

I believe that gaming is more than just playing — it's about connecting with awesome people, sharing laughs, and creating memories. My streams are a safe space for everyone to hang out, chat, and have a good time. ✨

The Journey

From a hobby gamer to a full-time content creator — every step of the way.

2020

The Beginning

Started gaming casually during lockdown, discovering a passion for streaming and connecting with other gamers online.

2021

First Stream

Went live for the first time on YouTube. Nervously played Valorant while chatting with the first few viewers who showed up.

2022

Community Growing

Streams started gaining traction. Built a loyal community of regulars who showed up for every stream. The vibes were immaculate.

2023

Going Full-Time

Made the leap to full-time content creation. Joined iSUPREME Gaming org for professional management and brand partnerships.

2024

Level Up

Expanded to multiple games, collaborations with fellow creators, and growing the HeyKhushi brand beyond just gaming.

Now

Creating Every Day

Streaming regularly, exploring new games, growing the community, and creating a life built around doing what I love. 🚀
